1033 53F-6-418. Private School Accreditation Work Group.
1034 (1) There is created the Private School Accreditation Work Group to establish a list of approved
accreditation organizations for purposes of Subsection 53F-6-401(15)(a)(ii)(F).
1036 (2) The work group consists of the following members:
1037 (a) the superintendent or the superintendent's designee;
1038 (b) the program manager or the program manager's designee;
1039 (c) two representatives from parent advocacy organizations, one whom the speaker of the House of
Representatives appoints and one whom the president of the Senate appoints;
1042 (d) two parents of scholarship students under the Utah Fits All Scholarship Program, whom the
superintendent appoints;
1044 (e) one representative from a private school that participates or intends to participate in the Utah Fits All
Scholarship Program, whom the superintendent appoints; and
1046 (f) one representative from a statewide nonprofit public policy research organization, whom the
superintendent appoints, that:
1048 (i) is headquartered in the state; and
1049 (ii) has published research or analysis on education choice programs or scholarship programs.
1051 (3) The work group shall:
1052 (a) hold its first meeting before July 1, 2026;
1053 (b) meet as necessary to fulfill the work group's duties under this section;
1054 (c) review and evaluate accrediting organizations;
1055 (d) establish criteria for determining which accrediting organizations provide adequate standards for
private schools participating in the Utah Fits All Scholarship Program; and

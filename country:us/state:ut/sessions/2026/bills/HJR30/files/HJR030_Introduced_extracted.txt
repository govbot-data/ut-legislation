20House Joint Resolution to Approve the Transfer of Land2026 GENERAL SESSIONSTATE OF UTAHChief Sponsor: Casey SniderSenate Sponsor: Scott D. SandallLONG TITLEGeneral Description:This resolution approves the transfer of state controlled lands. Highlighted Provisions:This resolution:acknowledges that the state and the United States government have disputed the ownership of 22,311 acres along the Great Salt Lake;describes the terms of a negotiated settlement between the state and United States government for the transfer of the disputed lands along the Great Salt Lake;describes the process for a state agency to obtain approval from the Legislature for the transfer of 500 or more acres of state controlled lands to the United States government; andapproves the transfer of 22,311 acres along the Great Salt Lake to the United States government.Money Appropriated in this Bill:NoneOther Special Clauses:NoneBe it resolved by the Legislature of the state of Utah:WHEREAS, the state of Utah and the United States government have disputed the ownership of 22,311 acres used for wildlife refuge along the Great Salt Lake;WHEREAS, the state of Utah through the Division of Forestry, Fire and State Lands, created in Utah Code Section 65A-1-4, has negotiated a settlement with the United States government that resolves the dispute of ownership;WHEREAS, the negotiated settlement provides for the transfer of 22,311 acres of state sovereign lands (acquired lands) to the United States government for agreed upon consideration to the state of Utah, which shall be used exclusively for the benefit of the Great Salt Lake;WHEREAS, the negotiated settlement provides that the United States government shall provide public use and access to the acquired lands consistent with federal law and regulation;WHEREAS, the negotiated settlement provides that the state of Utah reserves and retains all mineral interests, including subsurface and suspended minerals within the waters or brines of the Great Salt Lake; andWHEREAS, in accordance with Utah Code Subsection 63L-2-201(2), a state agency shall submit to the Legislature, for the Legislature's approval or rejection, an agreement to sell or transfer to the United States government 500 or more acres of state controlled lands:NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of the state of Utah approves the agreement negotiated by the Division of Forestry, Fire and State Lands for the transfer of 22,311 acres along the Great Salt Lake to the United States government.3-6-26 9:48 AM

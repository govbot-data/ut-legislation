1511-72-1010Online Marketplace Amendments2026 GENERAL SESSIONSTATE OF UTAHChief Sponsor: Lincoln FillmoreHouse Sponsor: Doug FiefiaLONG TITLEGeneral Description:This bill prohibits a municipality or county from regulating an online marketplace.Highlighted Provisions:This bill:defines terms; andprohibits a municipality or county from regulating an online marketplace.Money Appropriated in this Bill:NoneOther Special Clauses:NoneUtah Code Sections Affected:ENACTS:11-72-101, Utah Code Annotated 1953Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:Section 1. Section 11-72-101 is enacted to read:11-72-101. Online marketplaces.(1)As used in this section, "online marketplace" means a person that:(a)provides, for consideration, regardless of whether the consideration is deducted as a fee from a transaction, an online application, platform, program, software, system, website, or other medium through which a good or service is advertised in this state or is offered to the public as available in this state; and(b)provides, directly or indirectly, a system that:(i)facilitates a transaction between two persons;(ii)transmits or otherwise communicates the offer or acceptance of a transaction between two persons; or(iii)allows two or more persons to communicate or connect with each other.(2)A municipality or county may not regulate the operation of an online marketplace.Section 2.  Effective Date.This bill takes effect on May 6, 2026.1-8-26 3:19 PM

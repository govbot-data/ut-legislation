H.B. 404
Sex-Designated Housing Amendments
House Amendments Amendment 1 January 30, 2026 02:04 PM
Representative David Shallenberger proposes the following amendments:
1. Line 199 through 205:
199 (A) designate housing as single-sex housing; and
200 (B) limit occupancy of single-sex housing to individuals whose biological sex at
201 birth matches the designated sex for the single-sex housing.
202 (ii) {Section (5)(b)(i)} Section (6)(b)(i) applies to:
203 (A) a dormitory, a boarding house, a shared rental property, or other group-living
204 accommodation; and
205 (B) a private landlord and a property owner.

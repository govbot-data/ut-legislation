4 General Description:
5 This bill modifies provisions related to the release and forfeiture of an animal that is being
6 held because of abuse or involvement in a crime.

8 This bill:
9 ▸ provides a process to permit, while legal resolution of a crime is ongoing, the release or transfer
of an animal being held in a shelter because the animal was abused or was involved in a crime;

mistreated or is associated with a crime;

1 6 ▸ permits a shelter or government counsel to file a motion to have a court review whether an
animal being held in a shelter due to criminal activity should be forfeited or transferred to alternative

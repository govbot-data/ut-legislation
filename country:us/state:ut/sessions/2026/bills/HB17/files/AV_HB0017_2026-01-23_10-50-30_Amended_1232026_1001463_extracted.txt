617D-4-20517D-4-2053Public Infrastructure District Meeting Amendments2026 GENERAL SESSIONSTATE OF UTAHChief Sponsor: Doug WeltonSenate Sponsor: Brady BrammerLONG TITLE The Political Subdivisions Interim Committee recommended this bill.1412General Description:This bill requires public infrastructure district meetings be held at a location in the boundaries of the public infrastructure district subject to certain exceptions.Highlighted Provisions:This bill:requires a public infrastructure district board to hold meetings at a location in the boundaries of the public infrastructure district; andprovides exceptions that permit a board to hold meetings outside the boundaries of the public infrastructure district.Money Appropriated in this Bill:NoneOther Special Clauses:NoneUtah Code Sections Affected:AMENDS:17D-4-205, as renumbered and amended by Laws of Utah 2021, Chapter 314Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:Section 1. Section 17D-4-205 is amended to read:17D-4-205. Transparency -- Meetings of the board.(1)A public infrastructure district shall file annual reports with the creating entity regarding the public infrastructure district's actions as provided in the governing document.(2)(a)Notwithstanding Subsection 17B-1-310(2), and except as provided in Subsection (2)(b), a board shall hold regular and special meetings at a location within the boundaries of the public infrastructure district.(b)Subsection (2)(a) does not prohibit a board from holding a regular or special meeting at a location outside the boundaries of the public infrastructure district if:(i)(A)there are no locations in the public infrastructure district that are able to accommodate the board and the public that the board reasonably expects will attend the meeting; and(B)the location the board selects is near the public infrastructure district;(ii)it is not practicable to hold the regular or special meeting at a location within the boundaries of the public infrastructure district due to emergency circumstances; or(iii)the location the board selects is within the boundaries of the creating entity.(iii)(A)the meeting is a retreat held no more than once per calendar year;(B)the retreat is held for the purpose of planning, working, or education;(C)the board provides public notice and holds the retreat as a public meeting; and(D)at the meeting, the board does not take final action on any matter or vote on a motion other than a motion to adjourn.Section 2.  Effective Date.This bill takes effect on May 6, 2026.1-23-26 10:50 AM

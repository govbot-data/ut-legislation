125 Section 6. Section 6 is enacted to read:
127 23A-15-301. Grizzly bear management.
3. Grizzly Bear Management
121 (1) If the division discovers a grizzly bear in an area of the state where grizzly bears are listed as
threatened or endangered under the Endangered Species Act, the division shall contact the service
and request immediate removal of the grizzly bear from the state.
124 (2) The division shall manage grizzly bears to prevent the establishment of grizzly bears in any area of
the state until the grizzly bear is completely delisted under the Endangered Species Act and removed
from federal control in the entire state.
127 (3) Subsections (1) and (2) do not apply to a grizzly bear that is lawfully held in captivity and
restrained.

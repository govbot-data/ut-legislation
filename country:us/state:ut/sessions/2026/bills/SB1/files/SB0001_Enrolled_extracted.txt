PetersonLONG TITLEGeneral Description:This bill supplements or reduces appropriations otherwise provided for the support and operation of state government for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2025 and ending June 30, 2026 and appropriates funds for the support and operation of state government for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026 and ending June 30, 2027.Highlighted Provisions:This bill:provides appropriations for the use and support of higher education agencies and institutions;provides appropriations for other purposes as described;provides an estimate of the total budgets for higher education institutions; andprovides intent language.Money Appropriated in this Bill:This bill appropriates $44,508,000 in operating and capital budgets for fiscal year 2026, including:($261,300) from General Fund; and($7,444,000) from Income Tax Fund; and$52,213,300 from various sources as detailed in this bill.This bill appropriates $13,721,000 in restricted fund and account transfers for fiscal year 2026, all of which is from the various sources as detailed in this bill.This bill reflects $45,798,700 in higher education budget reporting for fiscal year 2026.This bill appropriates $3,082,927,300 in operating and capital budgets for fiscal year 2027, including:$485,504,300 from General Fund; and$1,289,593,900 from Income Tax Fund; and$1,307,829,100 from various sources as detailed in this bill.This bill appropriates $91,500,000 in restricted fund and account transfers for fiscal year 2027, all of which is from the Income Tax Fund.This bill reflects $12,952,223,200 in higher education budget reporting for fiscal year 2027.Other Special Clauses:This bill provides a special effective date.Uncodified Material Affected:ENACTS UNCODIFIED MATERIALBe it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:Section 1.

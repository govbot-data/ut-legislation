317D-2-3030Local Building Authority Amendments2026 GENERAL SESSIONSTATE OF UTAHChief Sponsor: Tiara AuxierSenate Sponsor: LONG TITLEGeneral Description:This bill amends the authority of a local building authority.Highlighted Provisions:This bill:prohibits a local building authority from executing a project previously rejected by the voters of certain creating local entities.Money Appropriated in this Bill:NoneOther Special Clauses:NoneUtah Code Sections Affected:ENACTS:17D-2-303, Utah Code Annotated 1953Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:Section 1. Section 17D-2-303 is enacted to read:17D-2-303. Voter-rejected project prohibited.A local building authority may not construct, acquire, improve, extend, or finance a project on behalf of the county, city, town, or school district that created the local building authority if the project, a portion of the project, or a substantially similar project, was rejected by the voters of a county, city, town, or school district.Section 2.  Effective Date.This bill takes effect on May 6, 2026.1-22-26 2:01 PM

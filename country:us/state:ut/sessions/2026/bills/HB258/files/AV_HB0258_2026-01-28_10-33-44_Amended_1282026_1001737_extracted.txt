Section 31A-22-663 is enacted to read:31A-22-663. Coverage for reversal of sex transition procedures.(1)As used in this section, "sex transition procedure" means:(a)hormonal transgender treatment as defined in Section 58-1-603;(b)a primary sex characteristic surgical procedure as defined in Section 58-67-102; or(c)a secondary sex characteristic surgical procedure as defined in Section 58-67-102.(2)A health benefit plan that covers any sex transition procedure shall also cover any procedure, treatment, or therapy necessary to reverse an individual's sex transition procedure.(3) This section applies to a health benefit plan renewed or entered into on or after January 1, 2027.Section 2.

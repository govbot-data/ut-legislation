20Concurrent Resolution Authorizing Adult Sentencing and Supervision Length Guidelines and Juvenile Disposition Guidelines2026 GENERAL SESSIONSTATE OF UTAHChief Sponsor: Tyler ClancySenate Sponsor: Todd WeilerLONG TITLEGeneral Description:This resolution authorizes the adult sentencing and supervision length guidelines and the juvenile disposition guidelines.Highlighted Provisions:This resolution:authorizes the adult sentencing and supervision length guidelines and the juvenile disposition guidelines; andestablishes the date on which the authorized adult sentencing and supervision length guidelines and juvenile disposition guidelines will take effect.Money Appropriated in this Bill:NoneOther Special Clauses:NoneBe it resolved by the Legislature of the state of Utah, the Governor concurring therein:WHEREAS, the Utah Sentencing Commission establishes and maintains the adult sentencing and supervision length guidelines that provide recommendations for sentencing and supervision for all criminal offenses and the juvenile disposition guidelines that provide recommendations and clarifications for practices and procedures for juvenile dispositions;WHEREAS, Utah Code Section 63M-7-406 requires the Legislature to annually authorize the adult sentencing and supervision length guidelines and the juvenile disposition guidelines; andWHEREAS, the Utah Sentencing Commission submitted the adult sentencing and supervision length guidelines and the juvenile disposition guidelines for authorization on February 12, 2026:NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature, the Governor concurring therein, authorizes the adult sentencing and supervision length guidelines and juvenile disposition guidelines submitted by the Utah Sentencing Commission on February 12, 2026.BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the authorized adult sentencing and supervision length guidelines and juvenile disposition guidelines shall take effect on May 6, 2026.2-27-26 3:40 PM

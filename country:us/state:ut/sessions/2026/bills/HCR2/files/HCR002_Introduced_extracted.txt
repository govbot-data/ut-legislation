2Concurrent Resolution Authorizing Adult Sentencing and Supervision Length Guidelines and Juvenile Disposition Guidelines2026 GENERAL SESSIONSTATE OF UTAHChief Sponsor: Tyler ClancySenate Sponsor: Todd WeilerLONG TITLECommittee Note: The Judiciary Interim Committee recommended this bill.Legislative Vote:13 voting for0 voting against3 absentGeneral Description:This resolution authorizes the adult sentencing and supervision length guidelines and the juvenile disposition guidelines.Highlighted Provisions:This resolution:authorizes the adult sentencing and supervision length guidelines and the juvenile disposition guidelines.Money Appropriated in this Bill:NoneOther Special Clauses:NoneBe it resolved by the Legislature of the state of Utah, the Governor concurring therein:WHEREAS, the Utah Sentencing Commission establishes and maintains the adult sentencing and supervision length guidelines that provide recommendations for sentencing and supervision for all criminal offenses and the juvenile disposition guidelines that provide recommendations and clarifications for practices and procedures for juvenile dispositions;WHEREAS, Utah Code Section 63M-7-406 requires the State Commission on Criminal and Juvenile Justice to annually submit the adult sentencing and supervision length guidelines and the juvenile disposition guidelines to the Law Enforcement and Criminal Justice Interim Committee and the Judiciary Interim Committee for review;WHEREAS, Utah Code Section 63M-7-406 requires the Legislature to annually authorize the adult sentencing and supervision length guidelines and the juvenile disposition guidelines submitted to the Law Enforcement and Criminal Justice Interim Committee and the Judiciary Interim Committee; andWHEREAS, the State Commission on Criminal and Juvenile Justice submitted the adult sentencing and supervision length guidelines and the juvenile disposition guidelines on October 29, 2025, to the Law Enforcement and Criminal Justice Interim Committee and the Judiciary Interim Committee for approval for the 2026 General Session:NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature, the Governor concurring therein, authorizes the adult sentencing and supervision length guidelines and juvenile disposition guidelines submitted to the Law Enforcement and Criminal Justice Interim Committee and the Judiciary Interim Committee.12-19-25 3:33 PM

2nd Sub. S.B. 120
Towing Modifications
House Amendments Amendment 1 February 11, 2026 09:06 AM
Representative Tiara Auxier proposes the following amendments:
1. Line 151 through 157:
151 (b) "Non-consent police generated tow" does not include the towing of a vehicle, vessel,
152 or outboard motor made at the request of a private individual where a peace officer
153 assists the tow request.
154 (2) (a) Except as described in Subsections (2)(b) { and } ,(c), and (d), a tow truck motor carrier
that
155 conducts a non-consent police generated tow may not tow the vehicle, vessel, or
156 outboard motor to an impound yard outside the county where the tow is initiated.
157 (b) For vehicle, vessel, or outboard motor impounded as described in Section 41-6a-1406
2. Line 167:
167 (ii) a peace officer, a person acting on behalf of a law enforcement agency, or a
168 highway authority informs the owner of the vehicle, vessel, or outboard motor of
169 the location of the impound yard.
(d) A tow truck motor carrier conducting a non-consent police generated tow may tow a
vehicle, vessel, or outboard motor to an impound yard outside the county where the tow was
initiated if:
(i) an impound yard within the county where the tow is initiated is unavailable; or
(ii) a tow truck motor carrier with an impound yard within the county where the tow
initiated cannot respond in a reasonable time due to weather, road conditions, or a volume of
requested tows.

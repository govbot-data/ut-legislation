164 (b) "Extended trip" means a school bus trip to a school-sponsored event, including an athletic
competition, academic competition, or club activity, that requires a student in grades 7 through 12 to
travel more than 60 minutes one-way.
167 (c) "Rural school district" means a school district that operates one or more eligible schools.
169 (2) Subject to legislative appropriations, the state board shall distribute grant money to rural school
districts to install and operate internet connectivity systems on school buses used for extended trips.
172 (3) A rural school district may apply to the state board for a grant under this section.

176 (b) submits an application in accordance with state board rule; and
177 (c) agrees to the requirements in Subsection (7).
178 (5) In awarding grants under this section, the state board shall give preference to rural school districts
that do not currently have internet connectivity systems on school buses used for extended trips.
181 (6) Notwithstanding Subsection (5):

receives funding for at least two school buses; and

189 (a) use grant money to:

191 (ii) provide internet access through a terrestrial wireless provider or a satellite internet service provider;

described in Subsection (7)(a)(ii) is expended;

years after receiving the grant;

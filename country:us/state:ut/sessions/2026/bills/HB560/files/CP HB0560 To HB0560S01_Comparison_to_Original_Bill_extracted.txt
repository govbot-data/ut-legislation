Subsection (2)(a):
1592 (i) the individual is not entitled to a prorated refund; and

receives notice of the individual's enrollment in the Medicaid program.

4. Enforcement
1660 (1) The commissioner may suspend or revoke a license issued under this chapter if the commissioner
determines that the ambulance membership organization:

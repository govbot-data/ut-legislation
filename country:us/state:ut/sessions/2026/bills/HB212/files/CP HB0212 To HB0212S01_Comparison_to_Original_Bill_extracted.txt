4 General Description:
5 This bill modifies provisions related to the creation of a new county.
6 Highlighted Provisions:
7 This bill:
8 ▸ defines terms;
9 ▸ establishes a process for the legislative body of a petitioning municipality to initiate a process to
create a new county;

91 (1) A municipality that establishes control of a prescriptive easement that is subject to a notice of intent

97 (2)
(a) A municipality may convert the use of the prescriptive easement established under Subsection (1) if
the municipality:
99 (i) mails a notice of the converted use of the easement to each landowner whose land is encumbered

101 (ii) files a notice describing the converted use of the easement in the office of the county recorder in
which the easement is located; and
103 (iii) waits 120 days from the day the municipality mails a notice under Subsection (2)(a)(i) before
beginning the converted use.

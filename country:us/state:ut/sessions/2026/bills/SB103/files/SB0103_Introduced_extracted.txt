353-3-1050Driver License Amendments for Unhoused Individuals2026 GENERAL SESSIONSTATE OF UTAHChief Sponsor: Jen PlumbHouse Sponsor: Stephanie GriciusLONG TITLEGeneral Description:This bill provides a fee waiver for an individual applying for a driver license who is experiencing homelessness.Highlighted Provisions:This bill:provides a fee waiver for an individual who is experiencing homelessness who is applying for a driver license;provides the verification needed for the application to qualify for the fee waiver; andmakes other technical changes for consistency.Money Appropriated in this Bill:NoneOther Special Clauses:NoneUtah Code Sections Affected:AMENDS:53-3-105, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2025, Chapter 471Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:Section 1.

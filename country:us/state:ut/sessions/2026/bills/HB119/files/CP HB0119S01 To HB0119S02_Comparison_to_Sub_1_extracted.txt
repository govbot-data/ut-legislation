4 General Description:
5 This bill amends provisions related to automotive repairs.
6 Highlighted Provisions:
7 This bill:
8 ▸ requires that a motor vehicle insurance policy contain a disclosure relating to the use of
aftermarket crash parts;
1 0 ▸ provides that coverage for a motor vehicle accident may not be stepped down under certain
circumstances;
1 2 ▸ requires that an insurer use crash parts that are substantially equivalent to original equipment
manufacturer aftermarket repair parts;
1 4 ▸ grants a motor vehicle owner a right of action against an insurer that does not use crash parts that
are substantially equivalent to original equipment manufacturer aftermarket repair parts; and
1 7 ▸ makes technical changes.

Section 23A-12-301 is amended to read:23A-12-301. Definitions.(1)The definitions in Section 23A-4-1201 apply to this part.(2)(a)As used in this part, "waterfowl management area" means real property owned or managed by the division that is:(i)primarily used for the conservation, production, or recreational harvest of ducks, mergansers, geese, brant, swans, and other waterfowl; and(ii)designated as a waterfowl management area by the Wildlife Board in accordance with Section 23A-12-303.(b)"Waterfowl management area" includes the following referenced in Section 23A-6-403:(i)Willard Spur Waterfowl Management Area;(ii)Harold Crane Waterfowl Management Area; and(iii)Bear River Bay Waterfowl Management Area.; and(iv)Goshen Bay Waterfowl Management Area.Section 3.

3 LONG TITLE
4 General Description:
5 This bill addresses water in the lower basin as defined by the Colorado River Compact.
6 Highlighted Provisions:
7 This bill:
1 1 ▸ exempts from provisions related to abandonment or forfeiture for nonuse certain water rights of a
water conservancy district that are related to the lower basin;
1 3 ▸ prohibits certain instream flow change applications related to the lower basin; {and}
1 1 ▸ provides a coordination clause between this bill and H.B. 348, Dedicated Water
Amendments, to include dedicated water applications in certain prohibitions of instream flow
applications related to the lower basin; and
1 4 ▸ makes technical and conforming amendments.
15 Money Appropriated in this Bill:
16 None
17 Other Special Clauses:
18 This bill provides coordination clauses.

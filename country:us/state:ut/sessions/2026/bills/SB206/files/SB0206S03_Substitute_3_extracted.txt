includes the following services: maintenance and enhancement of the statewide property tax system, including statewide web portals;provision of property valuation services;valuation of personal to pay off debt;requires an entity that receives tax increment to report annually to the program manager;modifies definitions relating to public service districts to reflect the transition of land

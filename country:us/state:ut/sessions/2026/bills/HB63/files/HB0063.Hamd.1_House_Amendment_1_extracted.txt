H.B. 63
Livestock Watering Amendments
House Amendments Amendment 1 January 22, 2026 12:20 PM
Representative Scott H. Chew proposes the following amendments:
1. Line 23 through 30:
23 (1) As used in this section:
24 (a) "Large pond" means a pond for livestock watering that is larger than a small pond.
25 (b) "Livestock" means a domestic animal raised or kept for profit or personal use.
26 (c) "Small pond" means a pond for livestock watering that has a {surface area equal to or }
27 {less than 900 square feet and a depth equal to or less than five feet} volume equal to or less
than two acre feet.
28 (d) "Sub-basin livestock watering claim" means a water right recognized to be used to
29 water livestock from existing surface water sources in a geographically defined
30 sub-drainage boundary approved by the state engineer.

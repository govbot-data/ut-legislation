819-2-109.80Pollution Standards Amendments2026 GENERAL SESSIONSTATE OF UTAHChief Sponsor: Andrew StoddardSenate Sponsor: LONG TITLEGeneral Description:This bill addresses pollution standards for particulate matter and nitrogen oxides.Highlighted Provisions:This bill:defines terms; andestablishes a total annual emissions limit for PM2.5 and nitrogen oxides from point sources in certain counties.Money Appropriated in this Bill:NoneOther Special Clauses:NoneUtah Code Sections Affected:ENACTS:19-2-109.8, Utah Code Annotated 1953Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:Section 1. Section 19-2-109.8 is enacted to read:19-2-109.8. Total annual emissions limit -- Particulate matter -- Nitrogen oxides.(1)As used in this section:(a)(i)"Major nonattainment county" means a county:(A)of the first or second class, as described in Section 17-60-104; and(B)in a nonattainment area, as that term is defined in 42 U.S.C. Sec. 7501, for ozone, PM2.5, or nitrogen oxides.(ii)"Major nonattainment county" includes:(A)Davis County;(B)Salt Lake County;(C)Utah County; and(D)Weber County.(b)"PM2.5" means airborne particulate matter approximately 2.5 micrometers or smaller in diameter.(2)The total annual emissions from point sources in major nonattainment counties may not exceed:(a)1418 tons of PM2.5 per year; and(b)8870 tons of nitrogen oxides per year.Section 2.  Effective Date.This bill takes effect on May 6, 2026.2-13-26 10:05 AM

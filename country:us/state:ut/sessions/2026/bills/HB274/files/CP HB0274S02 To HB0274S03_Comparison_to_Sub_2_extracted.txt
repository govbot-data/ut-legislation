7 This bill:
8 ▸ modifies the membership of the Sentencing Commission;
9 ▸ requires the Sentencing Commission to give consideration to certain objectives when

length guidelines;
1 2 ▸ requires the Sentencing Commission, before November 1, 2026, to revise and review the adult

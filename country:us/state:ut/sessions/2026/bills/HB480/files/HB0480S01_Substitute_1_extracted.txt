Section 26B-2-244 is enacted to read:26B-2-244Effective 05/06/26. Medical records.(1)An individual that has experienced a miscarriage or medically indicated abortion may request that a health care provider include in the individual's medical record and health history that the abortion was not an elective abortion.(2)A health care provider that receives a request described in Subsection (1) shall include a note in the individual's medical record.Section 3.

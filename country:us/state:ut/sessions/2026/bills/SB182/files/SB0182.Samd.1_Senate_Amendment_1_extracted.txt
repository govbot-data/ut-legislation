S.B. 182
Attorney Admission to Jail Facilities Amendments
Senate Amendments Amendment 1 February 06, 2026 04:15 PM
Senator Stephanie Pitcher proposes the following amendments:
1. Line 28 through 34:
28 (b) informs the sheriff that the attorney wishes to meet with a prisoner who is at the jail.
29 (2) An attorney may meet with a prisoner under Subsection (1) if the prisoner is:
30 (a) the attorney's current or former client;
31 (b) a prospective client and the meeting:
(i) was requested by the prisoner, the prisoner's family, or by a person on behalf of the
prisoner;or
(ii) the potential representation is for a civil rights matter;
32 (c) a potential witness to a criminal case that the attorney is prosecuting or defending; or
33 (d) an individual who may have information pertinent to a criminal case that the attorney
34 is prosecuting or defending.

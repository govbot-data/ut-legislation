H.B. 531
Scarification Amendments
House Amendments Amendment 1 February 18, 2026 04:34 PM
Representative Ashlee Matthews proposes the following amendments:
1. Line 96 through 104:
96 (1) Terms defined in Sections 76-1-101.5 and 76-9-2001 apply to this section.
97 (2) Except as provided in Subsection (5), an actor commits unlawful scarification of a
98 minor if the actor performs or offers to perform scarification:
99 (a) upon a minor; and
100 {(b) without receiving the consent of the minor's parent or legal guardian; and}
101 {(c)} (b) for remuneration or in the course of a business or profession.
102 (3) A violation of Subsection (2) is a class B misdemeanor.
103 (4) The owner or operator of a business in which a violation of Subsection (2) occurs is
104 subject to a civil penalty of $1,000 for each violation.

Public and Higher Education Collaboration
House Amendments Amendment 2 March 04, 2026 12:52 PM
Representative Val L. Peterson proposes the following amendments:
1. Line 36:
36 (1) "Education entity" means the same as that term is defined in Section 53E-9-301.

38 53H-1-101.
39 (3) (a) "Student data" means the same as that term is defined in Section 53E-9-301.
(b) "Student data" does not include information or records related to student behavior
incidents or student discipline.

SandallHouse Sponsor: LONG TITLEGeneral Description:This bill modifies property tax distribution for energy users.Highlighted Provisions:This bill:provides for distribution of property tax revenue collected from a property that has a cumulative electricity demand of 100 megawatts or greater within five years (large load customer) to taxing entities across the state;prohibits a taxing entity from awarding property tax increment to a project area that has a large load customer within the project area; provides the circumstances under which a large load customer shall notify the county auditor and county treasurer of the large load customer's address; andmakes technical and conforming changes.

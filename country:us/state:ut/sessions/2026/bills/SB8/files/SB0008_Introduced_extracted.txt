61State Agency and Higher Education Compensation Appropriations2026 GENERAL SESSIONSTATE OF UTAHChief Sponsor: Scott D. SandallHouse Sponsor: Walt BrooksLONG TITLEGeneral Description:This bill supplements or reduces appropriations otherwise provided for the support and operation of state government for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2025 and ending June 30, 2026 and for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026 and ending June 30, 2027.Highlighted Provisions:This bill:provides funding for a 1% labor market increase for state employees and higher education employees;provides funding for health and dental benefit cost changes as recommended by the Public Employees Health Programs;provides funding for retirement rate changes for certain state employees;provides funding for an up-to $26 per pay period match for qualifying state employees enrolled in a defined contribution plan;provides funding for other compensation adjustments as authorized; andprovides funding for step and lane increases for employees of the Utah Schools for the Deaf and the Blind.Money Appropriated in this Bill:This bill appropriates $7,941,000 in operating and capital budgets for fiscal year 2026, including:($313,700) from General Fund; and($1,314,200) from Income Tax Fund; and$9,568,900 from various sources as detailed in this bill.This bill appropriates $25,800 in expendable funds and accounts for fiscal year 2026, all of which is from the various sources as detailed in this bill.This bill appropriates $154,500 in business-like activities for fiscal year 2026, including:$200 from General Fund; and$154,300 from various sources as detailed in this bill.This bill appropriates $23,400 in restricted fund and account transfers for fiscal year 2026, all of which is from the General Fund.This bill appropriates $600 in fiduciary funds for fiscal year 2026, all of which is from the various sources as detailed in this bill.This bill appropriates $124,507,400 in operating and capital budgets for fiscal year 2027, including:$36,455,700 from General Fund; and$48,134,700 from Income Tax Fund; and$39,917,000 from various sources as detailed in this bill.This bill appropriates $179,900 in expendable funds and accounts for fiscal year 2027, including:$1,300 from General Fund; and$178,600 from various sources as detailed in this bill.This bill appropriates $449,500 in business-like activities for fiscal year 2027, including:$32,600 from General Fund; and$416,900 from various sources as detailed in this bill.This bill appropriates $600 in restricted fund and account transfers for fiscal year 2027, all of which is from the General Fund.This bill appropriates $41,100 in fiduciary funds for fiscal year 2027, all of which is from the various sources as detailed in this bill.Other Special Clauses:This bill provides a special effective date.Uncodified Material Affected:ENACTS UNCODIFIED MATERIALBe it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:Section 1.

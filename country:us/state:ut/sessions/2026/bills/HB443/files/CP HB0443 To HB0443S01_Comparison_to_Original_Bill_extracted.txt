1 0 ▸ establishes a procedure for calling a special election to fill a vacancy in the Legislature;

election or a special general election may be held to fill a vacancy in the Legislature; {and}

vacancy in the Legislature on a date other than a date that coincides with the dates of other elections{. }

election described in this bill.

4 General Description:
5 This bill addresses the unauthorized practice of law.
6 Highlighted Provisions:
7 This bill:
8 ▸ defines terms related to the unauthorized practice of law;
9 ▸ allows the court to take certain actions when a person is engaging in the unauthorized practice of
law before the court;
1 1 ▸ allows a civil action to be brought for the unauthorized practice law;
1 2 ▸ establishes a criminal penalty for the unauthorized practice of law;
1 3 ▸ clarifies that the statutory provisions regarding the unauthorized practice of law do not limit the
Supreme Court's authority to regulate the practice of law;
1 5 ▸ provides a three-year statute of limitations for a civil action for the unauthorized practice of law
and the exceptions to the statute of limitations;
1 7 ▸ reorganizes the three-year statute of limitations for certain actions; and
1 8 ▸ makes technical and conforming changes.

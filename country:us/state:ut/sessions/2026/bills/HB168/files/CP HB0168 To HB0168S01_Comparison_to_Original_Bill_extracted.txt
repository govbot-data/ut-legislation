603 Section 11. Section 11 is enacted to read:
604 23A-5-315.5. Unlawful activity by a pilot.
601 (1) As used in this section:
602 (a) "Aircraft" means an airplane or other airborne vehicle or device.
603 (b) "Pilot" means an individual who operates the controls of an aircraft while in flight.
604 (2) A pilot may not knowingly, intentionally, or recklessly operate an aircraft if the operation results in
a person taking, including locating, wildlife from the aircraft in violation of Section 23A-5-315.
607 (3) A violation of Subsection (2) is:
608 (a) a class B misdemeanor for the first violation;
609 (b) a class A misdemeanor for the second violation; or
610 (c) a third degree felony for a third or subsequent violation.
611 (4) If a pilot violates this section, the division may revoke a license, tag, certificate of registration, or
permit held by the pilot in accordance with Section 23A-4-1106.

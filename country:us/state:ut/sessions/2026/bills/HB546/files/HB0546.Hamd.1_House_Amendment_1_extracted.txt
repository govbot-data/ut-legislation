H.B. 546
Public Lands Duty of Care Amendments
House Amendments Amendment 1 February 27, 2026 09:19 AM
Representative Carl R. Albrecht proposes the following amendments:
1. Line 15 through 21:
15 →establish map layers for certain criteria on landscape-scale lands;
16 → map a landscape public nuisance area on landscape-scale lands containing forested
17 areas; and
18 →report annually on the map to the Federalism Commission and Natural Resources,
Agriculture, and Environment Interim Committee;
19 →requires the Division of Forestry, Fire, and State Lands to assist the office in identifying a
20 landscape public nuisance area for landscape-scale lands containing forested areas; and
21 →makes technical and conforming changes.
2. Line 293 through 299:
293 (b) coordinate with the division to identify polygons for any landscape public nuisance
294 areas based on criteria described in Section 63L-11-503.
295 (3) (a) No later than October 1 of each year, the office shall submit an annual report to
296 the Federalism Commission and the Natural Resources, Agriculture, and Environment Interim
Committee.
297 (b) The report described in Subsection (3)(a) shall include:
298 (i) updates to the digital map described in Subsection (1); and
299 (ii) recommendations for establishing zones for high value mineral areas.

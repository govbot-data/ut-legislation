McKellHouse Sponsor: LONG TITLEGeneral Description:This bill amends provisions related to kratom.Highlighted Provisions:This bill:prohibits the sale of kratom to an individual under 21 years old; andrequires the Controlled Substance Advisory Committee to issue a report on kratom products that are able to be sold in the state.Money Appropriated in this Bill:NoneOther Special Clauses:NoneUtah Code Sections Affected:AMENDS:4-45-105, as enacted by Laws of Utah 2019, Chapter 329ENACTS:58-38a-205, Utah Code Annotated 1953Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:Section 1. Section 4-45-105 is amended to read:4-45-105. Prohibition on sale to minors -- Criminal penalty.(1)A kratom processor may not distribute, sell, or offer for sale a kratom product to an individual under 1821 years of age.(2)A kratom processor who violates this section is guilty of a class C misdemeanor for each violation.Section 2. Section 58-38a-205 is enacted to read:58-38a-205. Kratom study.(1)As used in this section, "kratom product" means the same as that term is defined in Section 4-45-102.(2)The committee shall:(a)review scientific, medical, and regulatory information related to kratom products that comply with Section 4-45-104;(b)evaluate the safety profile, patterns of use, and appropriate regulatory framework for kratom products described in Subsection (2)(a); and(c)submit a written report, including recommendations for legislative action, to the Health and Human Services Interim Committee no later than January 15, 2027.Section 3.

Definitions.(1)As used in this part:(a)"Restricted Account" means the Corrections Facility Expansion Restricted Account created in Section 51-9-1002.(b)"State correctional facility" means a facility operated by the Department of Corrections to house inmates.Section 2. Section 51-9-1002 is enacted to read:51-9-1002. Corrections Facility Expansion Restricted Account.(1)There is created a restricted account within the General Fund known as the "Corrections Facility Expansion Restricted Account."(2)The restricted account shall consist of:(a)the portion of appropriations from new legislation for the Department of Corrections for:(i)any state correctional capital facilities costs; and(ii)one time savings that occur from delayed estimated fiscal impacts of increases in the population level of state correctional facilities;(b)interest, dividends, or other earnings attributable to the restricted account; and(c)additional money appropriated by the Legislature.(3)The Legislature may appropriate money from the restricted account only for the purpose of expanding existing, or constructing new, state correctional facilities.Section 3.

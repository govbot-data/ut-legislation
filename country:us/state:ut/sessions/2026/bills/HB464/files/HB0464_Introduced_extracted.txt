PeckSenate Sponsor: LONG TITLEGeneral Description:This bill allows individuals 15 years and six months old to obtain a driver license and allows parents to complete driver education requirements.Highlighted Provisions:This bill:allows individuals 15 years and six months old to apply for, and obtain a driver license;allows individuals 14 years and six months old to apply for, and obtain a learner permit;directs a local education agency to provide a parent with materials necessary to complete driver education classroom requirements for the parent's child;allows a parent to complete all the education requirements and behind-the-wheel driving hours for driver education purposes; creates new supervised driving hours requirements for parent taught driver education; andmakes technical changes.Money Appropriated in this Bill:NoneOther Special Clauses:NoneUtah Code Sections Affected:AMENDS:41-8-1, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2015, Chapter 41241-8-3, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2015, Chapter 41253-3-202, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2025, Chapter 22953-3-204, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2021, Chapter 26253-3-210.5, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2025, Chapter 24253-3-211, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2008, Chapter 31453-3-505.5, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2018, Chapter 41553G-10-502, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2021, Chapter 24753G-10-508, as last amended by Laws of Utah 2021, Chapter 247ENACTS:53G-10-509, Utah Code Annotated 1953Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah:Section 1.

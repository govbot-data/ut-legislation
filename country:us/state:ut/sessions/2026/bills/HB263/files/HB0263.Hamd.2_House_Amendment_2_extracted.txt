H.B. 263
Heavy Duty Vehicle Amendments
House Amendments Amendment 2 February 05, 2026 03:43 PM
Representative Ariel Defay proposes the following amendments:
1. Line 127 through 152:
127 Section 41-1a-1226. is enacted to read:
128 41-1a-1226 (Effective 05/06/26)Heavy duty emissions compliance fee -- Deposit.
129 (1) As used in this section:
130 {(a) (i)} (a) "High emissions heavy duty vehicle" means a motor vehicle that has:
131 {(A)} (i) a gross vehicle weight rating of more than 14,000 pounds; and
132 {(B)} (ii) a model year of 2009 or older.
133 {(ii)} (b) "High emissions heavy duty vehicle" does not include:
134 {(A)} (i) a farm tractor;
135 {(B)} (ii) a farm truck;
136 {(C)} (iii) an implement of husbandry;
137 {(D)} (iv) an ambulance;
138 {(E)} (v) a law enforcement vehicle;
139 {(F)} (vi) a fire engine; { or}
140 {(G)} (vii) a vehicle owned and used by the United States government or by the state or a
141 political subdivision of the state {.} ; or
(viii) a vehicle designed and used for the collection and transportation of municipal solid
waste and garbage.
142 {(b) (i) "Major nonattainment county" means a county:}
143 {(A) of the first or second class, as described in Section 17-60-104; and}
144 {(B) in an ozone nonattainment area, as that term is defined in 42 U.S.C. Sec. 7501.}
145 {(ii) "Major nonattainment county" includes:}
146 {(A) Davis County;}
147 {(B) Salt Lake County;}
148 {(C) Utah County; and}
149 {(D) Weber County.}
150 (2) Except as provided in Section 41-1a-1209, beginning on January 1, 2027, an applicant
151 shall pay a heavy duty emissions compliance fee in an amount of $175 at the time an
152 application is made for registration or renewal of a high emissions heavy duty vehicle.
